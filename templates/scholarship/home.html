About Layers of Truth Scholarship

The Layers of Truth Scholarship is designed exclusively for students at the Air Force Institute of Technology (AFIT), supporting those who exhibit academic excellence and come from diverse backgrounds. This initiative aims to reduce financial barriers and promote access to higher education.

  • Exclusively for AFIT students
  • Supports 50 students with financial aid and career guidance
  • Fosters diversity and academic excellence

Why Apply for the Layers of Truth Scholarship?

The Layers of Truth Scholarship provides more than just financial support. It opens doors to mentorship, career guidance, and opportunities that will shape your future.


Financial Support

Scholarship funds cover tuition, textbooks, and educational expenses for 50 deserving AFIT students.

Mentorship Opportunities

Selected students will have access to mentors who will guide them through their academic journey.

Career Guidance

We provide career guidance to help AFIT students achieve their professional goals after graduation.

Our Mission

At Layers of Truth Ministries, we are committed to guiding students on their educational journey by providing academic support and mentorship that fosters personal growth. We recognize the importance of nurturing both the mind and spirit, preparing students to succeed in their studies and beyond.

Why We Sponsor

As firm believers in the transformative power of education, Layers of Truth sponsors the AFIT scholarship to foster growth and create opportunities for students who are determined to succeed despite financial challenges. Our goal is to see students thrive and become leaders in their respective fields.
